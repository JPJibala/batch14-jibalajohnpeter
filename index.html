Bible Camp

Experience the adventure of a lifetime at Bible Camp 2024! From July 1-6, immerse yourself in a week of spiritual growth, exciting activities, and unforgettable friendships. Our camp is designed for all ages, offering engaging Bible studies, inspiring worship sessions, and interactive workshops that bring the Scriptures to life.

Discover the beauty of nature with outdoor adventures, team-building games, and evening bonfires under the stars. Whether you're deepening your faith or exploring it for the first time, Bible Camp 2024 is the perfect place to connect with God and others in a meaningful way.

Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to renew your spirit and create lasting memories. Register now and be part of an extraordinary journey at Bible Camp 2024!

Thanksgiving

Join us for a heartwarming Thanksgiving Celebration Extravaganza! Gather with friends, family, and neighbors on November 23rd from 3 PM to 8 PM at the beautiful Greenfield Park. Indulge in a bountiful feast featuring traditional favorites and delightful new dishes, all prepared with love and the freshest ingredients.

Enjoy live music, fun games for all ages, and a cozy bonfire to keep the festive spirit alive. Our event will also feature a community gratitude wall, where you can share what you’re thankful for this season.

Don’t miss out on this joyous occasion to create cherished memories and celebrate the blessings of the year. Everyone is welcome – bring your appetite and your gratitude!

Let’s give thanks together!
